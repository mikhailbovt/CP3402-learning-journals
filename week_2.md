## Week 2

## Learning Activities & Resources

This week, I learned about the fundamentals of Content Management Systems (CMS), including the differences between static and dynamic websites. I explored how web servers generate dynamic pages, reviewed common HTTP response status codes, and became familiar with the purpose and architecture of CMS platforms. I also started working with Joomla by creating the initial version of my website and customizing its appearance.

## Estimated Hours of Explicit Learning Activity

5 hours

## Content Insights

The most important insight this week was understanding why CMS platforms are widely used instead of static websites. A CMS separates content from presentation, allows multiple users to manage content efficiently, and makes websites much easier to maintain. I also learned how HTTP status codes help browsers and servers communicate during web requests.

## Career/Employability/Learning Insights

This week's activities improved my understanding of how modern websites are developed and maintained in professional environments. Learning to work with a CMS such as Joomla provides practical experience with technologies commonly used by businesses and highlights the importance of scalable, maintainable web development practices.
